About Me

I’m originally from Kharkiv, Ukraine, and I’ve been living in Poland for a little over two years now. Moving to a new country has been a big change, but it’s helped me grow both personally and professionally.

Right now, I’m in my second year at a technical school in Poland, where I’m studying informatics. My education focuses on programming, web development, and staying up to date with the fast-paced world of computer technologies. I really enjoy working with code, especially when I see an idea turn into something real on the screen.

Outside of school, I have a strong passion for 3D modeling. I’ve built a private collection of over 300 models and love experimenting with new techniques and tools, especially in Blender. I’m also a bit of a tech enthusiast—I enjoy tinkering with hardware, testing peripherals, and understanding how everything fits together.

But what really makes me different is my love for confectionery. Creating desserts and cakes is my creative outlet. Whether it’s baking something special for friends or just trying out a new decoration style, I find a lot of joy in it.

One quote that really speaks to me is: “You can think of me however you wish, but I will never become who you think I am.” It reminds me to stay true to myself, even when others try to put me in a box.
